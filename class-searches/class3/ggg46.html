Byoomao LY-G d11-9374 A 4

GGG #46

Discovered By: CMDR Cyanidex

Date: 3309-02-01

Description:

This Class III GGG has a dark beige-tan base cloud color, dominated by bright hazy pink clouds. Bioluminescent green clouds wrap the planet around its equatorial regions. Within its rings are Notable Stellar Phenomena sites containing K10-Type Anomalies. This is the second GGG to be found in the BYOOMAO sector.
