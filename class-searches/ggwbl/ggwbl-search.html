Water-Based Life

Green Gas Giants with Water-Based Life are the most infamous amongst the known GGG classes for many reasons. The majority of them share a dark red base cloud layer, which is quite starkly contrasted by the usually massive bands and streaks of green clouds. Due to many different cloud banding patterns possible on these planets make each one distinct from the other. Other colors that can be found on them are bright pink, a dark tan color, and an even darker red, almost a black color. Please note that due to a codex bug, this planet class is unable to be reported to the codex. Its 'green' codex slot is instead erroneously taken by Green Gas Giants with Ammonia-Based Life. The first Green Gas Giant ever discovered was a Gas Giant with Water-Based Life, which was previously lost, and then found seven years later
